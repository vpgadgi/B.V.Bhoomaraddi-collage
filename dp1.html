About the Department

The Department was established in the year 1960 and has produced excellent students who are now working in various fields. The young faculty members are dedicated to understanding students and guiding them towards a bright future. The department boasts a separate library with 200 books, and there are 9308 books available in the college central library. In total, the department has published 24 books, 16 articles, and won 04 state and regional level awards.

Objectives of the Department

  1. To develop a rich culture and tradition through Kannada literature.
  2. To simplify teaching by using modern technology.
  3. Creating interest in the heritage of Kannada literature.

Future Plan

  1. Organize an International level Conference.
  2. Invite eminent Professors from various National Universities to conduct special lectures.
  3. Establish Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Kannada University Hampi and Janapada University Haveri.
  4. Seek funds from Government Organizations for research activities.
