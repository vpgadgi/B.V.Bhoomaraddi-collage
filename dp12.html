About the Department

The Department was established in the year 1960. It has its separate computer lab with advanced software Maxima available for teaching and learning. The academic programs offered by the department include B.Sc.

Objectives of the Department

  1. Provide students with a strong foundation to identify, analyze, and solve real-life problems.
  2. Encourage students to undertake further study of basic science courses at the research level.

Future Plan

To organize International level Seminars & Conferences. Develop Research activities with funding agencies.
