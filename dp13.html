About the Department

Computer Science Department was established in the year 1990 with Bachelor of Comp Science combinations of PMCS, CME and PECS. Later in the year 2001, BCA Bachelor of Computer Application was introduced. It is a Computer Oriented and Professional Course having 3 year undergraduate Degree Programme that focuses on the basics of computer application and software development. A BCA degree is considered to be at par with a B.Tech./B.E. degree in computer science or information technology.

Objectives of the Department

  1. Create successful IT graduates who can solve real-life problems with computing skills.
  2. Encourage students to undertake software development.
  3. Excel in competitive examinations.
  4. Inculcate professional culture among students to become successful industry-oriented graduates.
  5. Seek admission in higher learning.

Future Plan

To start a certificate course in Data Analytics in association with the IT industry. To tie up with local industries for professional software development training. To foster research activities among staff and students. Visit industry research centres and academic institutions. To facilitate acquiring additional certificates and value-added courses. To improve placement.
