About the Department

The Department of Zoology was established in the year 1962. The Department has two young highly qualified teaching faculty members. The Department has a systematic strategy for the implementation of the curriculum. It also has a well-equipped laboratory with necessary models such as a projector, microscope, binocular microscope, and a well-maintained museum.
