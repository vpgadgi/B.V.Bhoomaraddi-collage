About the Department

Our department was established in the year 1980. The course offered is UG affiliated to Gulbarga University Kalabugri. The department has two Faculty members and one supporting staff. Our department has projectors installed in the classroom to enable the teachers to give PowerPoint presentations and audio-visual presentations. The students also use this facility to give their presentations on subjects that facilitate learning and presentation skills. Our department has been equipped with ICT that has rendered Teaching Learning method more interesting and interactive. Our department has two spacious well-equipped laboratories with the latest equipment. The departmental library has several books on the field of electronics from basic to reference books mentioned in the syllabus.

Objectives of the Department

  • To provide knowledge of Basic electronics devices and their application in daily life.
  • To impart knowledge and skills so as to inspire the students for innovation & creativity, leading to a progressive career in industry.
  • Impart quality education to meet the challenges of the industry and enhance employability of the students by providing skills through experiential learning.

Future Plan

  • Organizing practical workshops with industrial experts.
  • To establish better tie-ups with reputed industries and thereby enhance the placement of students.
  • Plan to have MOU with research institutes/industries.
  • Motivate the students to take up research activities.
  • Organizing skill-based training programs and job-oriented programs.
  • Student Enrichment programs.
