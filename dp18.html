About the Library

College Library was established in the year of 1960. The College Library is on the ground floor in a space of 4980.8 sqft with a separate reference, periodical, competitive books section and so on. There are 58,312 Books, 13 Newspapers, 18 magazines, Journals available for students & staff to go through, there is also a book bank facility for the students belonging to SC/ST and other minorities.

Objectives of the Department

  • To acquire, organize and update the library collection to support the teaching-learning process.
  • To encourage students to read beyond the requirements of the curriculum.
  • To work with faculty in integrating information skills, knowledge of information sources, and the use of technology in accessing information needed to strengthen their instruction, research and extension.
  • To provide instruction and assistance in the effective use of learning resources/services.

Future Plan

  • Department level Book Exhibition.
  • Audio-Video Facility to the students.
  • Extension networking libraries with higher education institutions.
  • Plan to implement Digital Library Software like Dspace, Eprint, etc.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each student will be issued 2 to 4 books, Identity card and uniform are compulsory for getting the books from the library. Students should obey the rules and regulations.
  • When you are entering the library, you have to show your Identity Card.
  • Every day student has to sign in the library attendance register.
  • You have to maintain complete silence in the library. You can read books, magazines, newspapers, etc., in the library without disturbing others.
  • Books are issued on the borrower’s card only for 15 days. If you want again you have to renew or return the book. Keeping without renewal you have to pay the fine of Rs.1/- per day.
  • Students can borrow other than your subject books on the borrower’s card like grammar, fictions, poetries, etc.
  • When you are getting books from the library, you have to check books before leaving the library. You have to show any damages, written, or disfigured in the book to our library staff.
  • The students sometimes lost the book or books you have to replace the same book or pay the amount double in price.
  • Reference books, question papers, magazines, newspapers, journals are not issued outside the library.
  • If students need any information please ask the Librarian.
  • Make the best use of the library, “Let us read books yourself, and give a chance to others”.
  • Identity card is necessary for internet browsing.
