About the Department

Objectives

  1. To help students acquire knowledge of English Language and literature.
  2. Creative and critical response to literature in light of cultural values and literary History.
  3. To provide a platform to the students to exhibit their talents and creativity.
  4. To raise the standards of our students to global levels and make them skillful.
  5. To make the students competent and develop a positive attitude.

Future Plan

  1. Conduct International and National Conference/Seminars.
  2. To involve a greater number of students in class presentations and seminars.
  3. Meet eminent Professors, writers, Journalist of English.
  4. To visit university and institute libraries.
  5. Encouraging students to write articles, poems, short stories, dramas, etc.
