About the Department

The Department of Hindi was established in the year 1960. The Hindi department is running a graduate program in Hindi in the jurisdiction of Gulbarga University, Kalaburgi. The department has a qualified and experienced staff with a good teaching profile.

Objectives of the Department

  1. To Create An Interest In the Hindi Language And Literature.
  2. To Master the art of Communication.
  3. Hindi is the national language of India, so students should be motivated to study this language.
  4. This literacy art will establish ethical values in students.
  5. To Develop Excellent Proficiency in the Hindi Language.

Future Plan

  1. To Organize National/International Conference/Seminar/Workshop.
  2. To Start Certificate Course in ‘Translation’.
  3. Planning to conduct more Special Lectures by inviting Professors from various Institutions.
  4. Planning to publish articles/Poems from students.
