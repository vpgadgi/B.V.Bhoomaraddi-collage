About the Department

Our department was established in the year 1960 with optional facilities. We offer B.A., B.Sc., B.Com., and B.C.A. courses with Urdu as one of the basic subjects in combination. In addition to the basic Urdu subject, we also offer optional subjects.

Objectives of the Department

  1. To spread awareness about the syncretism and synergetic genius and importance of Urdu culture Language and Literature.
  2. To protect and promote creativity and originality.

Future Plan

  1. Improving the student strength of the department.
  2. To organize National and International level seminars.
  3. Preparing a more interesting and job-oriented syllabus for the future of the students.
