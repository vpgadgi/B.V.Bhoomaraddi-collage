About the Department

Our department was established in the year 1960. Prof. S.G. Bhatt was the First staff member; later, he joined the Planning Commission, Govt. of Karnataka. Prof. V.C. Somasundaram served the department for 35 years and worked as District Commandant of Home Guards. Prof. S.M. Kulkarni of the department served as Assistant Registrar for 5 Years at Gulbarga University, Kalaburagi. Prof. Basavaraj Ghasne, Prof. S. R. Reddy, Prof. Vamanrao Kulkarni strengthened the department.

Objectives of the Department

  1. To achieve a higher level of excellence.
  2. Provide wider opportunities in Industries, Govt. sectors, Banks, LIC, and other monetary institutions.
  3. Promote higher studies, research, and job opportunities.
  4. Develop entrepreneurship skills.
  5. Impart knowledge of Economic activities pertaining to Economic Development.
  6. Provide relevant practical learning experience.

Future Plan

  1. To organize National and International Seminars.
  2. To undertake projects for community purposes.
  3. To organize programs for personality development.
  4. To organize more field trips and study tours.
