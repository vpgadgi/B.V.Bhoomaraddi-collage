About the Department

Our department was established in 1960, and it is one of the vibrant departments in the college. The department strives for promoting awareness of social issues apart from delivering curricular aspects.

Objectives of the Department

  1. To create political consciousness and empower students in the Hyderabad-Karnataka region to be capable people.
  2. To realize high ideals of justice, equality, liberty, and fraternity.
  3. To promote awareness of legitimate governance and human rights.
  4. To equip and empower students with relevant knowledge, competence, and creativity to face national challenges.

Future Plan

  1. Survey by the staff and students of local bodies department (Taluka Panchayat, Zilla Panchayat) on any problem confronting the people.
  2. Visit to assembly or parliament during sessions at least once in a year.
