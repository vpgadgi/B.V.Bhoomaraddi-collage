About the department:

Our department came into existence in 1960, with Dr. L. R. Kulkarni as the first H.O.D. Pioneers of our department include Prof. M. S. Adake, Smt. N. T. Gangamma, and Prof. G. M. Kamanatagi. The current faculty members have vast experience in teaching and research. The department features a museum and a library with 100 books. Besides central library facilities, the department conducts National and International level Seminars, Special Lecturer Programs, and has MoUs with various colleges in Bidar District to expose students to different cultures.

Objectives of the department:

  1. Prepare students for the challenging situations of life.
  2. Develop a spirit of Nationalism.
  3. Foster academic studies in History.
  4. Cultivate interest and enthusiasm for the History of this region.

Future plan:

  • Encourage students to take up internship programs in archaeology.
  • Motivate students to engage in research projects on European, Middle Eastern, East Asian, and Indian Histories.
  • Organize National and International Level Seminars.
  • Plan more field visits.
  • Encourage students to work on projects recreating historical monuments.
  • Start programs that raise awareness among students about competitive exams.
