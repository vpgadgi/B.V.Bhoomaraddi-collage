History

Collage History

Bidar, a part of Hyderabad Karnataka area, remained economically and educationally backward area inspite of having rich cultural background. Our founder President, Late Shri.Mahadevappa Rampure did a yeoman service by realizing this and started B.V.B. College, in the year 1960 to provide educational opportunities to the youth of this backward area and made their educational dream come true. People stopped going to Hyderabad or Gulbarga for higher education. He was a great visionary, the 52 acres of land purchased in 1960’s is ample proof of his vision. This College is named after the famous philanthropist Shri.B.V. Bhoomaraddi from Gadag. It marked a new era in Bidar’s educational history. The College is affiliated to Gulbarga University Kalaburagi. It offers Six Semester Degree Course in Arts, Science, Commerce and Computer Application. Apart from this College offers four semesters M.Sc. Course in Computer Science.
