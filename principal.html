Principal's Message

Congratulations to all admission seekers! You are indeed fortunate to consider the option of pursuing six semesters UG or four semesters PG Course offered by B. V. Bhoomaraddi UG and PG College, Bidar. This will be a historical beginning of innovative path breaking exercise contemplated by B. V. Bhoomaraddi UG and PG College in its pursuits for enabling its students to face the ever increasing challenges of our globalized society. This UG & PG programmes aims at giving students a firm grounding in their respective disciplines while also acquainting them with skills for critical thinking. WhatsApp_Image_2023-02-27_at_2.35.17_PM The various discipline centered six semester programmes for UG and four semester programme for PG offered by our college has been given in detail in this Admission Brochure. These inbuilt foundation courses are expected to further enhance the overall confidence level and personality of the students. I assure all the stakeholders that, our College does not compromise on academic quality, students discipline and safety. We have been putting continuous efforts in further improving our College infrastructure. In addition Academic cum amenities blocks are completed with the required number of class rooms, ICT class rooms, Seminar hall, Laboratories, Wi-Fi facility, Language Laboratory, Library, Canteen, Health and Fitness Club (Gymnasium), Health Center, Play Ground, Safe drinking water (RO System), NSS, NCC, Scouts & Guides, students centric activities, cultural activities and other amenities are also been put in operation. CCTV Cameras installed in the College campus to achieve our objects.

Yours sincerely,

Dr. P.Vithal Reddy

M.Sc.,ph.D
